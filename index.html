Jardim Gramacho

The largest landfill in Latin America.

History

This talks about where Jardim Gramacho was built and mentions Christ the Redeemer statue.

  • The site receives more trash every day than any landfill in the world.
  • 7,000 tons of garbage arrive daily making up 70% of the trash produced by Rio de Janeiro and surrounding areas.

It was started in 1970 and later people called catadores began living and working there, picking recyclable materials to sell.
